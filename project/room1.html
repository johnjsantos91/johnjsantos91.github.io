Missing and Murdered Indigenous Women

In 2016, Out 5,712 reports of missing American Indian and Alaska Native women and girls, only 116 cases were logged.

About 46% of indigenous women have experienced some sort of physical abuse including rape and domestic violence.

96% of sexual violence against Native women were committed by non-natives
