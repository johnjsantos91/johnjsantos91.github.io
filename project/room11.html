Indian Land Tenure Foundation

Community-based organization serving American Indian nations in the recovery and control of three homelands.

Indian Land Tenure Foundation provides funding to Indian nations to help them recover their land.

Indian Land Tenure Foundation supports a variety initiatives to assist tribes in reacquiring reservation lands.

Promotes education, increase culture awareness, and create economic opportunity.
