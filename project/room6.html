Health Care Inequality

Despite legal obligations of the United States to provide Health care to Native Americans, They still lag behind compared to other groups.

Native Americans have a life expectancy that is 4.4 years less than other races in the United States.

Indigenous peoples experience disproportionately high levels of maternal and infant mortality, malnutrition, HIV/AIDS and diseases such as tuberculosis and diabetes.
