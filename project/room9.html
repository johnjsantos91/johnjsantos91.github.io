Native American Heritage Association (NAHA)

A nonprofit organization that provides needs of impoverished Native Americans.

Provides basic life necessities including clothing, food, fuel and stove provisions for winter heating and emergency vouchers

Works in partnership with Feeding America to provide nutritious food to reservations

Delivers approximately 350,00lbs of food that you would normally purchase at the grocery store each month.

Clothing donations is one of NAHA's successful programs. They deliver around 25,000lbs of clothing to reservations per month
