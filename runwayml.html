RunwayML AdaIN/Style Gan2


The objective of this project is to transform images using RunwayML Style GAN2 and AdaIN-Style Transfer.

My series of RunwayML images are about futuristic landscapes. I used two landscape art pieces from my favorite illustrator and combined them together using RunwayML to create different sci-fi like effects.


Mediums Used:​​ RunwayML (AdaIN-Stle Transfer + GAN2)
